Why I'm a Good Fit For Your Company

Why does anyone do anything? I'd say that the answer to that lies in our base human needs and desires. You sit in your cushy job where you get to decide who makes a living at your company. With but a pen stroke, you control the lives of all the applicants who are desperate enough to seek work with you

That's where I come in. Haven't you had enough looking through the applicants? There's a good chance that it doesn't even matter who you hire. It's not like you get a bonus if, say, 3 years down the line, I'm a more productive worker than the other guy might have been. No, who you hire probably won't affect you in the slightest. Wouldn't it be easier to just hire me? Just pretend to have read all the other applications and mark me down as hired. It's that easy. You can spend the rest of your day relaxing. Wouldn't that be nice?
